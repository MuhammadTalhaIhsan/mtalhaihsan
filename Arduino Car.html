Arduino Car

Skills :

SolidWorks

Description :

For my IMechE Design Challenge project, I designed and built a small car powered by an Arduino. The goal was to create a vehicle that could perform simple, repeatable tasks on a straight track. The car used basic components like motors, sensors, and batteries, focusing on creating an efficient and reliable design that met the competition's guidelines.

I began by developing several design options and selecting the best one based on performance and practicality. After finalizing the design, I assembled the car using an Arduino, motors, sensors, and a circuit board. I used SolidWorks to create a detailed 3D model of the car, and Tinkercad to plan and simulate the circuit before building it, ensuring all components worked together smoothly.

This project helped me improve my skills in electronics, programming, and design. With the combination of Arduino, SolidWorks, and Tinkercad, I successfully created a functional car that was ready for the competition and met all design requirements.
